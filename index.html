A Griffin's Night of Glamour

Spend a Night in the Roaring 20's!

May 2, 2020;  7pm-11pm

Pricing

The Junior class here at  North Wake will sell tickets to both Juniors and Seniors who wish to attend Prom. This will be a joint event between NWCCA, VMCCA, & WakeECHS high schools.

 
Attire

The attire for the night will be formal and students are encouraged to dress according to the theme of the event; the Roaring 20's. Please keep in mind that a dress code will be enforced. Still curious? Further details can be found here.

 
Location

The venue for North Wake's prom will be held at Marbles Kids Museum. Please visit Google Maps for further location directions.

 
Parent Sign-Up Form

Parents! Want to be a part of this memorable night? Your assistance will be needed and very much appreciated. Click here for the link to the sign up form.
